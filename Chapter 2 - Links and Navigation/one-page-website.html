Holyland

A quick review of an underrated fighting manga.

Overview

Holyland is a psychological martial arts manga written and illustrated by Kouji Mori. It follows Kamishiro Yuu, an outcast high school student who is alienated by the environment he finds himself in. But through his discovery of his own strength through street fighting, he carves out a place for himself in the violent world of the streets.

Key Information

  • Genres: Action, Psychological, Martial Arts
  • Written & Illustrated by: Kouji Mori
  • Published by: Young Animal Magazine
  • Original Run: 2000 - 2008
  • Volumes Released: 18

Creator

Kouji Mori: A manga artist known for his deep, psychological storytelling and realistic portrayal of martial arts. Aside from Holyland, he is also recognized for works like Suicide Island and Destroy and Revolution.

Characters

Kamishiro Yuu: The protagonist, a timid high school student who becomes a skilled street fighter through his determination and practice.

Masaki Izawa: A legendary street fighter who acts as Yuu's mentor and helps guide him through the brutal world of underground fights.

Shougo Kirishima: A powerful and respected fighter, known for his discipline and control in battle.

Shin: A ruthless gang leader who challenges Yuu's place in the streets.
