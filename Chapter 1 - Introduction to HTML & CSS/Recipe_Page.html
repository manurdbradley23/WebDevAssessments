How to Make Chicken Cordon Bleu

A cordon bleu is a dish wherein a dish of meat pounded thin, filled up with ham and cheese, breaded, and then either baked or panfried.
In this blog, we will go through the cooking process step by step in order to make this delicacy.

Ingredients:

  • Panko breadcrumbs - 1/2 Cup
  • Oil spray
  • 2 small chicken breasts - 6-7oz
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4 slices swiss cheese
  • 4 to 6 slices of ham
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 3 1/2 dijon mustard
  • 1 1/2 tbsp butter
  • 1 1/2 tbsp flour
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 3 tbsp parmesan cheese, finely grated

The cooking process:

  1. Preheat oven to 200C/390F or 180C/350F
  2. Spread panko breadcrumbs on a baking tray and spray with oil. Bake for 3 minutes until light golden. Remove and scrape the crumbs into a bowl.
  3. Cut a pocket into each chicken breast.
  4. Fold the cheese in half and place 2 pieces inside each chicken. Do the same with the ham, close the pocket, and seal with 2 toothpicks.
  5. Mix the 3tbsp mayonnaise and 1 1/2 dijon mustard with salt and pepper in a bowl. Spread onto the top and sides of the chicken.
  6. Sprinkle panko over the chicken, and press breadcrumbs onto the side of the chicken, spray with oil.
  7. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes until golden brown and cooked. Remove the toothpicks after.
  8. Melt butter over medium heat in a small saucepan. Add flour and cook for 1 minute
  9. Add half the milk and whisk until the flour mixture is blended in.
  10. Add remaining milk, mustard and cheese. Cook for 3 minutes, whisking constantly, until thickened. It will thicken as it cools.
  11. Remove from heat, add salt and pepper to taste. Serve with chicken
  12. Serve the Chicken Cordon Bleu alongside with the Dijon Cream Sauce.
